Ann claims that Mark hit her car on Wednesday evening, 
while he was exiting a parking lot. 
However, Mark's passport irrefutably show that he was abroad that week.
Select which of the following statements are true:

(Select all acceptable answers.)
A. Mark might have had a car accident.
B. Mark never had a car accident.
C. Mark's car was in an accident.
D. Mark may never have had a car accident.
E. If Mark had been at the parking lot that Wednesday evening, then Ann's claim would be true.
F. On Wednesday, Mark exited a parking lot.

Correct answers: C, D, E
